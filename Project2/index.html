This website talks about a photo tour of Bicol, Philippines, a beautiful place full of amazing sights and adventures. This is where I grew up and spent most of the childhood and teenage years. This whole province includes countless of beautiful sceneries and places that you can visit. It shows many nice spots where people can take great pictures, like the famous Mayon Volcano with its perfect cone shape. This webiste also includes information about Cagsawa Ruins, where you can see old church walls with Mayon in the background. Another beautiful spot is Bato Falls, a calm and lovely place to unwind and be one with the nature. Visitors can also enjoy the beautiful beaches in Caramoan, and tasty local food such as Bicol Express. People from around the world should visit Bicol to see its natural beauty. It is a great place to explore, take pictures, and make special memories. Come join us, as we explore the different tourist destination in the best region of the Philippines. BICOL!!!

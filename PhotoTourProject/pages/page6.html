Foods in the Bicol Region

Bicol Express

Bicol Express is a popular Filipino dish known for its rich, creamy, and spicy flavor. It features
tender pork cooked in coconut milk, combined with chili peppers and shrimp paste, creating a savory
and flavorful stew. This dish is best enjoyed with steamed rice, making it a hearty and satisfying meal.

Laing

Laing is a well-known dish from Bicol made with dried taro leaves cooked in coconut milk, chili,
and sometimes meat or seafood. It has a rich, creamy, and spicy taste and is usually served with rice.
Laing is loved for its unique flavor and soft texture.

Pancit Bato

Pancit Bato a unique noodle dish from Bato, Camarines Sur in Bicol. What makes it special is
the local noodles, which are firmer and chewier than regular pancit. These noodles are often stir-fried
with vegetables, meat, and soy sauce, giving it a tasty and homemade flavor. It is a proud local dish known
for its simple yet delicious taste.

Quitinday Green Hills

Want a breathtaking view of the Mayon Volcano together with a range of green hills? Visit the
Quitinday Green Hills in Bicol, Philippines. This place looks like a smaller version
of the Chocolate Hills, but with fresh green grass and a view of the famous Mayon Volcano in the
background. It is a peaceful spot where you can enjoy nature, take great photos, and feel the fresh air.
The hills are easy to climb, and once you're at the top, the view is wide and beautiful. It is a great place
for people who love nature, quiet spots, and amazing scenery.

    • All about Mayon Volcano

    Mayon Volcano is a beautiful and famous volcano in the Philippines, known for its almost perfect cone shape. It stands tall and proud, with green forests around its base and a smooth, pointed top. The volcano is active, which means it can erupt, and sometimes it sends out smoke, lava, and ash. Even though it can be dangerous, many people visit to see its amazing shape and stunning views. Mayon is a natural wonder that shows both the beauty and power of nature.

    Cagsawa Ruins

    Cagsawa Ruins is the best place to take a picture of Mayon Volcano because it shows both the old church and the volcano together. The ruins of the church are quiet and beautiful, and they tell a story from the past. Behind them, you can see the tall and perfect shape of Mayon Volcano. The view is clear and wide, so it is easy to take a good photo. On sunny days, the sky is blue, and the volcano looks even more amazing. The mix of history and nature makes Cagsawa Ruins a perfect and special spot for pictures.

    Activities you can do

    • ATV Ride Adventure: Ride an all-terrain vehicle near the volcano.
    • Hike or Trek: Go hiking on the slopes or nearby trails for a closer view of the volcano.
    • Take Photos: Capture beautiful pictures of the volcano from different angles.

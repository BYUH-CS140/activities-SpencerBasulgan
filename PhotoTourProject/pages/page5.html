Hoyop Hoyopan Cave

Tired of all the greens and blues of the Bicol region? If you're looking for something to trigger
your adrenaline, visit Hoyop-Hoyopan Cave. This cave is found in Albay and is full of amazing rock shapes
and cool paths to explore. Inside, it feels like a different world, with wide spaces, narrow tunnels, and
interesting walls made by nature. The name “Hoyop-Hoyopan” means “blow-blow” because of the wind that flows
through the cave. It is a fun place for people who enjoy walking, adventure, and discovering new things. It is
also a great way to take a break from the usual beach and mountain views.
